Somehow serious ...

Hello, everybody, I'm @JayGwod (Guo Dejie), an Algorithm Engineer and a Musician (at least I think so 😆). Currently I'm studying in Peking University as a PhD student majoring Intelligence Science, and previously I worked as an Algorithm Engineer at Lingban Technology.

Actually I had no idea about I would have any relationship with Computer Science just several years ago. At that time, my dream was to become a great Composer and my career was all about music and arts. I graduated from Communication University of China and then got a master degree of Arts in Music from China Conservatory of Music. Meanwhile, I also took a job at China National Theatre for Children as a Sound Designer to chase my music dream.

Now I believe that Arts and Science are the same thing. Not only would I never give up the dream of Music, but also I'm trying to use the techniques of Artificial Intelligence to help me achieve this goal. If you're also interested in the combination of Arts and Science, welcome to contact me 👉GitHub·JayGwod. Let's build some cool stuff together💪
